Sports asset range

NeoTrack groups its sports assets into clear categories so that academies and institutions can plan complete kits for each game.

Field sports equipment

Assets for outdoor games like football, cricket and athletics, designed for school and college grounds as well as professional academies.

  • Football goal posts, corner flags and practice cones.
  • Cricket kits, boundary ropes, sight screens and score boards.
  • Athletics hurdles, starting blocks and relay batons.

Indoor sports and fitness

Equipment for badminton, table tennis, basketball practice, and fitness zones inside campuses and sports complexes.

  • Badminton and volleyball nets with poles and court markers.
  • Table tennis tables, rackets and training ball packs.
  • Gym mats, dumbbells, resistance bands and agility ladders.

Stadium & event support

Supporting assets needed to run tournaments and cultural events in the same arena, from seating to sound.

  • Portable player benches and spectator seating blocks.
  • PA systems, microphones and basic lighting rigs.
  • Registration desks, barricades and signage stands.

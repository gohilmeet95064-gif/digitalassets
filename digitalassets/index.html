Professional sports assets for modern facilities

NeoTrack Sports Assets is a concept store in Ahmedabad that supplies and digitally tracks professional sports equipment for schools, colleges, academies and stadiums. The focus is on organised inventory and long‑term use of every asset. [web:149]

What does NeoTrack offer?

The shop brings together high‑quality sports assets such as kits, goal posts, nets, mats, timing systems and stadium accessories, and combines them with a simple digital system so that every item can be located and monitored easily. [web:158]

  • Multi‑sport equipment for indoor and outdoor games.
  • Assets designed for schools, colleges and training academies.
  • Inventory‑friendly packaging with clear IDs and labels.

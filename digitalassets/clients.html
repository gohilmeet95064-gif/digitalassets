Where are NeoTrack assets used?

The NeoTrack concept is designed for institutions that manage shared sports infrastructure and need reliable equipment over many seasons. [web:149]

Typical client profiles

Client type Examples of assets Key requirement
Schools and colleges Basic multi‑sport kits, indoor games, fitness zones. Durable equipment that is easy to issue and collect from students.
Sports academies Advanced training gear, timing systems, practice nets. High‑performance assets with clear tracking for batches and camps.
Stadiums and clubs Stadium seating, benches, sound systems, event accessories. Fast setup for tournaments and cultural events on the same ground.

Usage across seasons

With a digital record of assets, NeoTrack can show which items are heavily used during school sports day, inter‑college tournaments or summer coaching camps, helping managers plan purchases and maintenance. [web:134][web:149]

Digital system for tracking assets

NeoTrack combines physical sports assets with a basic digital tracking system so that every kit, post or mat can be identified, located and maintained without confusion.

How NeoTrack tagging works

  • Unique ID for every asset: Each item gets an ID like NT‑FB‑005 for the fifth football kit.
  • Category and location: The ID is linked with category (field, indoor, event) and storage or ground location.
  • Issue and return log: Whenever a school or academy borrows assets, the quantity and date are recorded.
  • Condition notes: Basic remarks capture wear and tear to plan repair or replacement.
